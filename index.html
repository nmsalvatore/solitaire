STACKS & SHORTCUTS

Click a card and everything stacked on top of it comes along for the ride. Double-click any card to send it straight to its foundation.

UNDO

Hit UNDO or ⌘Z to undo — keep pressing to rewind as far as you want.

GAME MODES

LAZY / SISYPHUS — in SISYPHUS you pick and place every card yourself. LAZY lets you just click a card and it moves automatically.
DRAW 1 / DRAW 3 — flip one card at a time, or three for a challenge. Switching starts a new game.
